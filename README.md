## 🛠 How to Enable Two-Step Verification

### Introduction
Two-step verification is an optional feature that adds more security to your WhatsApp account. When you enable it, any attempt to verify your phone number on WhatsApp must be accompanied by the 6-digit PIN that you created.

### Prerequisites
* An active WhatsApp account.
* A mobile device with the latest version of WhatsApp installed.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Open **WhatsApp** on your mobile device.
2. Go to **Settings**. 
   > *Note: On Android, tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top right corner.*
3. Navigate to **Account** > **Two-step verification**.
4. Tap **Turn on**.
5. Enter a **6-digit PIN** of your choice and confirm it.
6. **(Recommended)** Provide an email address to reset your PIN if you forget it.
7. Tap **Done**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> WhatsApp will periodically ask you to enter your PIN to help you remember it.
